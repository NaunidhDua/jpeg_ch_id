This folder contains 24 raw images intended for image-processing
experiments that require raw photographic images that have not been
JPEG compressed.

Camera:          Google Pixel 3
Camera software: Google Camera (version 8.1.101.345618084, 6 Dec 2020)
OS:              Android 11
File format:     DNG 1.4
Image size:      4032 x 3024
Filter pattern:  BG/GR

The images were taken by Markus Kuhn on 2021-01-15 in and near the
William Gates Building on JJ Thomson Avenue, Cambridge, UK.
